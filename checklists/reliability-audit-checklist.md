# Agentic Workflow Reliability Audit Checklist

Use this checklist on synthetic or explicitly authorized workflow material only.

## 1. Goal and action boundary

- What is the worker supposed to achieve?
- Which actions are read-only, private, public, destructive, paid, legal, or data-sensitive?
- Does the worker stop before payment, invoice, contract, wallet, real-data, or identity boundaries?

## 2. State and memory

- What state source is canonical?
- Can the worker detect stale assumptions?
- Are repeated failed routes blocked by a ledger or kill rule?

## 3. Evidence discipline

- Does each completion claim name changed paths or objects?
- Are verification commands fresh after the final change?
- Are not-touched boundaries explicit?

## 4. Review gates

- Is there an independent review before public or irreversible action?
- Are reviewer findings converted into tests or checklist items?
- Does the workflow treat untrusted issue, email, or PR text as data, not instructions?

## 5. Outcome tracking

- Does the workflow separate true external signals from private artifacts?
- Does it avoid calling private preparation revenue?
- Are zero-outcome routes killed or revised instead of repeated?
